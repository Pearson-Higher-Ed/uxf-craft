{% include '_includes/designSys/shared/levelFour' with { 'topSpacing': true, 'name': 'Times', 'desc': 'Keep the following best practices in mind when writing about time: ' } %}
  • Use the 12-hour clock, followed by am or pm.
  • Don't put a space after the last number, as in 7:00 am. Write it as 7:00am.
  • Specify noon or midnight for 12:00 in parentheses for clarity, as in 12:00 (midnight).

When it's necessary to indicate a time zone, use the time zone abbreviation after am or pm.

To indicate a time range, use an en dash with spaces around it and include am/pm after both times.

Use the browser time of the logged in user.
