{% include '_includes/designSys/shared/levelFour' with { 'name': 'Times', 'desc': 'Keep the following best practices in mind when writing about time: ' } %}
  • Use the 12-hour clock, followed by AM or PM in uppercase letters.
  • Put a space after the last number, as in 7:10 AM.
  • When there are no minutes, write the time without them, as in 7 AM EST.
  • Specify noon or midnight in parentheses for clarity, as in 12 AM (midnight).

When it's necessary to indicate a time zone, use the time zone abbreviation after AM or PM.

4:30 PM GMT

To indicate a time range, use an en dash with spaces around it and include AM/PM after both times.

3:30 PM - 4:30 PM

Use the browser time of the logged in user.

{% include '_includes/designSys/shared/levelFour' with { 'name': 'Abbreviations for days, hours, and minutes', 'desc': "Use the following abbreviations for days, hours, and minutes:" } %}

d = day/days

h = hour/hours

min = minute/minutes
